Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score total to the randomized number. You lose if the total score exceeds the randomized number.

The values of each crystal are hidden until the you click on it.

A randomized number is generated after each time you win or lose.
